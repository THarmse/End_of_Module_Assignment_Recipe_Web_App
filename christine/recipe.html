Ingredients

4 people

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g beef mince
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 400g spaghetti
  • grated parmesan, to serve

65 mins

4 people

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large, heavy-based saucepan with a lid and fry the onion and carrot for 5 mins. Add the garlic and cook for a further min.
  2. Add the beef and cook on a high heat until browned all over. Stir in the tomatoes, tomato puree, stock cube, oregano, bay leaves and 200ml water. Bring to a simmer and then reduce the heat. Partly cover with a lid and cook for 1 hr, stirring occasionally.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti following pack instructions. Serve with the bolognese and some grated parmesan.
